From WikiHow:

    1. Pinch your fingers and thumbs together in front of your chest 
        4 times.
    2. Flap your arms four times.
    3. Wiggle side to side four times while getting your back side as 
        close to the ground as you can.
    4. Clap four times.
    5. Repeat steps 1-4 until you hear the swing-like music.
    6. Swing around with a partner or group until the swing-like music 
        ends. Start back at the beginning.
